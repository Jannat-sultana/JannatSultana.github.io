About Me

Hello! I am Jannat Sultana, a Computer Science and Engineering graduate. I obtained my BS in CSE from North South University (NSU), Dhaka, Bangladesh. During my undergraduate studies, I had the privilege of working as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant at NSU. Currently, I am working as both a Graduate Teaching Assistant and Graduate Research Assistant at NSU. I am working on projects funded by ECE department of NSU under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Rashedur M. Rahman.

My Research Focus: I am deeply passionate about developing efficient machine learning systems and advancing natural language processing capabilities. My work centers on optimizing supervised learning algorithms and pretrained language models for enhanced computational efficiency, with applications spanning Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision.

Beyond the world of algorithms and data, I find inspiration in traveling to new places and immersing myself in music-experiences that fuel my creativity and provide fresh perspectives on problem-solving.
